About

CollegeGuru is an online educational institution that guides Nepalese high school graduates to get into US universities for a Bachelor’s degree. The program runs for six months from the month of July to December, and provides students with a complete package for US college application. This package includes TOEFL class, SAT class, College Essay Writing Help, Talks, Workshops, and Extra-curricular development. CollegeGuru is currently run by students and graduates from several universities in the US including MIT and UC Berkeley.
