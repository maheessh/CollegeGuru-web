About

CollegeGuru is an online educational institution that guides Nepalese high school students and graduates to get into US universities for a Bachelor’s degree, aiming for partial to full scholarships. The program runs for six months from the month of June to January, and provides students with a complete package for US college application. This package includes SAT class, College Essay Writing, Duolingo Classes, Talks, Workshops, and Extra-curricular development. CollegeGuru is currently run by students and graduates from several universities in the US including MIT and UC Berkeley.
